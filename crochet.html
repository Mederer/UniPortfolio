Crochet

I know, crocheting is definitely not an IT skill. However, I hope that the picture of my work can give you a glimpse into my growing creativity, as well as my passion for constantly seeking out new skills to learn.

Currently my goal is to make a large blanket, which I've been told is the perfect project for a beginner.

At the moment I'm creating small squares called "granny squares". Once I have enough of them, my intention is to sew them all together along the edges to make a nice fluffy blanket.

My ultimate goal is to learn how to crochet plushies. Collecting plushies has been a hobby of fine since I was a child, and I'd love to be able to make my own some day.
